BioMedData (Life Sciences) LookUp is an initiative that aims to aid your research data management. It is designed to help you understand data standards, tools, and resources relevant to your life sciences research domain and therefore provides context that can help you manage data in a Findable, Accessible, Interopraable, and Resuable (FAIR) way. Our primary audience is a community of current and future life scientists in Norway.
